## Key Excerpts

**Litmus Edge Overview:**
- Edge computing platform for collecting, normalizing, and forwarding industrial data from factory floor equipment to cloud or on-premise analytics systems
- "An Industrial DataOps company that enables industrial companies to unlock and activate their operations data at scale, with technology that is rapid-to-deploy, easy-to-use and built-to-scale"

**Protocol Support:**
- 250+ industrial protocol drivers including: OPC UA, Modbus, Ethernet/IP, PROFINET, MQTT, S7, Fanuc FOCAS, MTConnect, and hundreds more

**Edge Processing Capabilities:**
- Containerized applications via Docker/Kubernetes at the edge
- Can deploy custom applications: quality checks, local ML inference, protocol translation directly on Litmus Edge device
- Data normalization layer: converts diverse OT protocols to unified MQTT/REST output

**Azure IoT Operations Partnership (November 2024):**
- Litmus Edge Bridge for Azure IoT Operations automates industrial IoT device discovery and schema modeling
- Integration automates industrial data onboarding and modeling, accelerates OT-IT collaboration at scale

**Cloud Integrations:**
- Pre-built connectors for: AWS IoT SiteWise, Azure IoT Hub, Google Cloud IoT, Snowflake, and more

**Enterprise Customers:**
- Parker Hannifin, Dell Technologies, HPE, Mitsubishi, and other Fortune 500 companies

**Key Differentiator (per 2026 review at MachineCDN):**
- 250+ protocol drivers is highest in class for legacy equipment support
- Container-based architecture allows custom edge apps alongside connectivity
