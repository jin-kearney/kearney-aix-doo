## Key Excerpts

> OT/IT integration maturity is the highest-weighted dimension for manufacturing — it determines which AI use cases are technically feasible.

> A 2025 Gartner survey found that 61% of manufacturers rate their OT/IT integration as "basic" or "non-existent," capping AI maturity at Stage 1 regardless of data science capability.

> Industrial Internet Consortium benchmarks show AI-ready factories need 80%+ sensor coverage on critical assets.

> A Gartner study found that 58% of manufacturing AI pilot delays stem from infrastructure readiness, not model development.

## Data Infrastructure Evaluation Criteria

- Sensor coverage density
- Data pipeline reliability
- Format standardization (OPC-UA adoption vs. legacy protocols)
- Historical data depth
- Edge computing capability

## Key Benchmark

- **80%+ sensor coverage** on critical assets = AI-ready factory threshold (Industrial Internet Consortium)
- Sub-second data pipeline latency for real-time use cases

## Assessment Framework

A manufacturing-specific AI readiness assessment evaluates eight dimensions with sector-appropriate criteria and weighting, typically taking 2–3 weeks, producing a scored readiness profile with prioritized gap-closing actions.
