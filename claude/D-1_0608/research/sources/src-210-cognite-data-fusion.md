## Key Excerpts

**Overview:**
- Cognite Data Fusion (CDF): industrial data platform designed to connect, contextualize, and organize industrial data into a unified structure
- "An open, secure Industrial Data and AI platform that enables quick deployment of a contextualized data foundation for rapid scaling of AI-powered digital solutions"
- Contextually fuses IT, OT, ET and other data types and makes it available to all data consumers

**Data Contextualization:**
- Ingests data from different systems and organizes it into a contextualized data layer
- Contextual layer supports: digital twins, advanced analytics, machine learning models, operational dashboards
- "A New Way: why industrial digitalization needs data fusion and contextualization"

**Data Preparation and AI Integration:**
- Data Workflows: robust solution for increasing the scalability and observability of industrial data
- Automates contextualization of 360-degree images
- Generative AI tooling for industrial data management and routine task automation
- Use AI Agents to orchestrate industrial data pipelines (per blog post 2025)

**Practical Benefits:**
- Find the right data up to 12x faster
- Understand data without needing subject matter expert support
- Develop AI solutions up to 25x faster using reusable templates

**Asset-Centric Model:**
- Organizes all data (time-series sensor readings, documents, P&IDs, maintenance records) around physical assets
- Links sensor tags to equipment hierarchy in a knowledge graph structure

**Industries:**
- Heavy manufacturing, oil and gas, energy, mining, offshore
- Originally developed for offshore oil and gas (Equinor/Statoil), now expanded to broader industrial manufacturing
