## Key Excerpts (from search summary)

> "Prompt Drift is the unintended divergence of production prompts from their registered or intended versions, caused by manual edits, deployment pipeline overwrites, or copy-paste errors across environments."

> "Copy-paste proliferation occurs when someone shares a working prompt in Slack, others modify their local copies, and five slightly different versions end up in production."

## Notes
PE Collective is a prompt engineering community. Definition is precise and useful for explaining silent breakage mechanism.
