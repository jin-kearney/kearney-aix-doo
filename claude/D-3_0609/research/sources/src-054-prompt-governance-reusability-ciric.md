## Key Excerpts

> "In the era of generative AI, prompts have become a critical resource for organizations leveraging large language models (LLMs) to automate, innovate, and derive insights. Much like data, prompts represent valuable assets that require effective governance to maximize their utility, ensure compliance with organizational policies, and minimize costs."

> "Reusable prompts act as predefined templates that ensure consistency and efficiency in interacting with LLMs. When optimized and governed effectively, they can provide significant savings and promote organizational alignment."

> "Prompt Stewards: Similar to data stewards, these individuals oversee the creation and refinement of prompts. They ensure prompts align with organizational standards and remain relevant to business needs. Prompt Committees: Cross-functional committees establish policies for prompt usage and resolve disputes over their appropriateness."

> "Prompt Cataloging: Centralized repositories serve as libraries where prompts are categorized by function, department, or use case, ensuring accessibility and discoverability. Lifecycle Management: Prompts, like data, require periodic reviews to stay relevant."

> "Prompt governance parallels data governance in its focus on people, processes, and technology."

> "I've seen teams save hours of repetitive work by simply centralizing their most-used prompts. It's not just about saving time — it's about empowering employees to focus on higher-value activities."
