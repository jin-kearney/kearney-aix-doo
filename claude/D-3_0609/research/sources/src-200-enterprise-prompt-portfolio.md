## Key Excerpts

> "Rather than viewing prompts as ad-hoc code snippets, organizations might consider applying proven enterprise asset management principles to govern, optimize, and scale AI interactions. This approach would treat prompts as valuable intellectual property requiring structured lifecycle management, quality assurance, and strategic alignment."

> "**Prompt reuse rate across departments: Industry benchmark 40–60%**" (Suggested Portfolio-Level Strategic Metrics Extension)

> "Time-to-production for new AI capabilities: Target reduction from weeks to days"

> "Percentage of business processes incorporating AI assistance: Target 80% within 24 months"

> "Organizations could adapt their existing software development practices to include prompts: Using semantic versioning (X.Y.Z) to track major, minor, and patch updates."

> "Conduct comprehensive prompt asset discovery across business units; Establish governance structure with defined roles and responsibilities; Assess current processes and select appropriate tooling; Begin baseline performance measurement and documentation." (Phase 1: Assessment and Foundation, Months 1–3)

> "Launch cross-departmental reuse initiatives targeting 40% reuse rate; Enable non-technical stakeholders to edit and test prompts directly." (Phase 3, Months 7–12)

> "Research analyzing enterprise AI users found that the average prompt editing session lasted 43.3 minutes, with approximately 50 seconds between prompt versions, highlighting the highly iterative nature of prompt development. 93% of sessions involved parameter or model tweaks beyond just prompt text changes."

> "Most organizations lack: Centralized Repositories, Quality Standards, Performance Measurement."
