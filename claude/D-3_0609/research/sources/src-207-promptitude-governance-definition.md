## Key Excerpts

> "Prompt governance is the organized process of managing, reviewing, and updating the instructions given to AI systems, ensuring they remain accurate, safe, and aligned with company policies."

> "Prompt governance involves overseeing the entire lifecycle of prompts used in AI, from creation to retirement. This includes regular reviews, updates, and monitoring to ensure prompts are clear, effective, and follow organizational guidelines."

> Key aspects include:
> - Creating and updating prompts
> - Monitoring for quality and security
> - **Retiring outdated or incorrect prompts**

> "A customer service team reviews chatbot prompts monthly to ensure responses are up-to-date."

> "Compliance teams approve new prompts before they go live, reducing risk and ensuring regulatory standards are met."

> Healthcare example: "Outdated prompts are retired, ensuring the AI always provides current, approved information to staff and patients."

> "Understanding and applying prompt governance is crucial for keeping AI systems trustworthy, accurate, and safe. It helps organizations avoid mistakes, maintain compliance, and deliver reliable results to users."
